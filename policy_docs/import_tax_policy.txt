Sri Lanka Vehicle Import Tax Regulations (2024-2026):
- Electric Vehicles (EVs) below 100kW motor capacity enjoy a concessionary excise duty rate of 35%.
- Petrol vehicles below 1000cc carry a standard customs duty of 120% to 150% depending on CIF value.
- Vehicles older than 3 years from Year of Manufacture (YOM) are prohibited from commercial import into Sri Lanka.
